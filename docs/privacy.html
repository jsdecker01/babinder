Privacy Policy

How Babinder handles your data

Last updated: December 2025

Data We Collect

Babinder collects minimal data necessary to provide the service:

  • Swipe data: Which names you liked or disliked, stored to find matches with your partner
  • Household code: A random code that links you with your partner
  • Anonymous user ID: A random identifier to distinguish between household members

How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your swipes with your partner
  • To identify matches (names you both liked)
  • To provide statistics about your swiping activity

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Apple's iCloud (CloudKit). We do not have access to your iCloud account or any other personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your swipe data is only shared with members of your household.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings > Reset All Data in the app. This removes all your swipes and matches from our servers.

No Personal Information

Babinder does not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
